Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, of meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minnutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minuts

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables., cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, whisk the eggs until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Step 2: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Step 3: Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, tilting the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Step 4: As the eggs begin to set, gently lift the edges with a spatula, allowing uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  5. Step 5: If desired, add your choice of filling on one half of the omelette.
  6. Step 6: Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, fold it in half over the filling.
  7. Step 7: Cook for another minute or until the omelette is fully cooked and the filling is heated through.
  8. Step 8: Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately. Enjoy!
